## CHESS RULES REFERENCE

### 1. Piece Movement

| Piece  | Movement |
|--------|----------|
| King   | 1 square in any direction |
| Queen  | Any distance horizontally, vertically, or diagonally |
| Rook   | Any distance horizontally or vertically |
| Bishop | Any distance diagonally |
| Knight | L-shape (2+1 squares); the ONLY piece that can jump over others |
| Pawn   | 1 square forward (2 on first move); captures 1 square diagonally forward; cannot move or capture backward |

### 2. Special Moves

- **Castling**: King moves 2 squares toward a Rook; the Rook hops over. Both must be unmoved, path must be clear, and the king must not pass through or land on an attacked square.
- **En Passant**: Pawn capture allowed immediately after the opponent moves a pawn 2 squares forward from its starting position.
- **Promotion**: A pawn reaching the 8th (or 1st) rank MUST be replaced by Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight.

### 3. Game States & Termination

- **Check**: King is under direct attack.
- **Checkmate**: King is in Check and has no legal escape → Win/Loss.
- **Stalemate**: Current player is NOT in Check but has no legal moves → Draw.
- **Draw conditions**: 50-move rule, Insufficient Material, Threefold Repetition.

## AI DECISION PRIORITY (STRICT ORDER)

1. **Checkmate** — Execute a checkmate move immediately.
2. **Avoid Mate** — Escape or block an opponent's checkmate threat.
3. **Check Escape** — If in check, you MUST escape (move king, block, or capture attacker).
4. **High-Value Capture** — Capture a higher-value piece with a lower-value piece (e.g., Pawn takes Queen).
5. **Hanging Pieces** — Capture any undefended enemy piece.
6. **Favorable Trade** — Exchange pieces for material or positional advantage.
7. **Center Control** — Occupy or attack central squares (d4, d5, e4, e5).

---

## GAME PHASE OVERVIEW

- **Opening**: Develop pieces efficiently, contest center squares (e4/d4/e5/d5), castle early for king safety.
- **Middlegame**: Identify threats and tactics (Checks, Captures, Threats — CCT). Execute your plan.
- **Endgame**: Activate the king, push passed pawns, simplify the position when ahead.
